Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kelimenator Global ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information directly. All vocabulary progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to show personalized ads. This may include:

  • Advertising ID (GAID)
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

3. Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data only on your device:

  • Your study progress and statistics
  • Selected language pairs and app settings

This data is never transmitted to any server and is removed when the app is uninstalled.

4. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET – Required to load advertisements.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – Used by AdMob to check connectivity before loading ads.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob – Advertising

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
